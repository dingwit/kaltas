FAQs

Are resistance bands effective for building muscle?

Yes! Resistance bands create constant tension on muscles, promoting strength and muscle growth. Studies show they can be as effective as free weights when used correctly.

How long does a resistance band last?

With proper care, resistance bands can last 6 months to 2 years, depending on usage and material quality.

Are resistance bands safe for beginners?

Yes! They are beginner-friendly and low impact, reducing the risk of injuries. Always start with proper form and gradually increase resistance.
